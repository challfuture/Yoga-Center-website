Welcome to YogaMe

YogaMe is a great way to start you day, evenings and nights. We offers everything from full classes to tutorials to meditation practices. We have a wide variety of trained instructors and yoga styles, which Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin, Kundalini, and Ashtanga as well as power and prenatal classes.

Mindfulness Meditation is one of our most popular classes. Our Mindfulness Based-Stress Reduction (MBSR) classes helps the individual to focus on the mind.

We have now added a new class to our schedule called Calm. Clam is a base on a number of spiritual techquies given by our teachers from around the world.

The pace of our yoga class is very gentle. It includes asana demonstration, the opportunity to stop and ask questions, and extra individual attention. This is a one-hour-and-45-minute class.
